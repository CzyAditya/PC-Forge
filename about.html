About PC Forge

A modern, framework-free PC builder built as a showcase of clean HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Our Mission

PC Forge exists to make custom PC planning accessible to everyone — from first-time builders to seasoned overclockers. No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Just a fast, beautiful tool that helps you pick the right parts the first time.

Key Features

⚡

Live Pricing

Totals update the moment you change a part.

🛡️

Compatibility Checks

Socket, RAM type and PSU wattage validation.

📊

Performance Rating

Each part has a 0–100 performance score that rolls up to your build.

💾

Local Storage Builds

🆚

Fully Responsive
